Game Rules

This is a turn-base strategy game. The objective of the game is to make your opponent's lifepoints to 0 to win the game.
  • Each turn you can go to combat to try to deal damage to your opponent. If you win the combat, you deal damage equal to your attack power. If you lose, your opponent deals damage to you equal to his attack power.
  • During your turn, if you win, you have a chance to increase your attribute. You can choose to increase your attack power, hit chance or regen.
  • Attack Power increases your damage in combat.
  • Hit chance increases your chance of winning in combat.
  • And Regen increases your regen amount. Every turn after combat, you have a "chance" to gain lifepoints equal to your regen attribute.
  • After combat on your turn, if your lifepoints is 15 or less, you can activate your Special.
  • Gain 3 AP special adds 3 points to your Attack power.
  • Gain 3 HC special adds 3 points to your Hit chance.
  • Gain 3 Regen special adds 3 points to your Regen.
